- Brown meat in butter in a pan and Instapot on saute, add salt and pepper.
- Transfer pan meat to Instapot.
- Add to the Instapot:
  - 3 cups beef stock
  - 3 tbsp jar garlic
  - 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- Pressure cook for 25 min.
- While pressure cooking, dice onion.
- Add onion and mushrooms to pan, brown.
- Set aside browned veggies, keep warm.
- When pressure cooking is done, open and add veggies.
- Deglaze pan with white wine, add that to the Instapot.
- Simmer in Instapot with lid off.
