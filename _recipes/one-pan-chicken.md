---
title: One-Pan Chicken
yield: Serves three.
ingredientsToBuy:
  - { qty: "2 lb", item: "Chicken breast cutlets (boneless)" }
  - { qty: "1 lb", item: "Fingerling potatoes" }
  - { qty: "2 cans", item: "Chopped green beans" }
ingredientsYouHave:
  - Olive/canola oil
  - Butter
  - Garlic powder
  - Dried minced onion
  - Montreal seasoning
equipment:
  - { type: "2", item: "Baking sheets w/ metal grid" }
  - { type: "1", item: "Med stainless steel mixing bowl" }
---
- Preheat oven on Convection Bake to 425.
- Use the slicer to cut the potatoes into thin coins.
- Cut the chicken into small pieces.
- Toss each separately in oil and Montreal seasoning.
- Distribute evenly into baking sheet.
- Top with grated parmesan cheese.
- Bake for 25 min until potatoes are tender and chicken reaches 165 deg.
- Serve with BBQ sauce and/or Creamy Jalepeño sauce.
