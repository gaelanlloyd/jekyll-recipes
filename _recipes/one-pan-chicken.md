---
title: One-Pan Chicken
yield: Serves three.
ingredientsToBuy:
  - { qty: "2 lb", item: "Chicken breast cutlets (boneless)" }
  - { qty: "1 lb", item: "Fingerling potatoes" }
  - { qty: "1 pkg", item: "Green beans (frozen)" }
ingredientsYouHave:
  - Olive oil
  - Butter
  - Garlic powder
  - Dried minced onion
  - Montreal seasoning
equipment:
  - { type: "Large", item: "Baking sheet w/ metal grid" }
  - { type: "1", item: "Med stainless steel mixing bowl" }
---
- Melt 4 tbsp butter in the crucible.
- Preheat oven on Convection Bake to 425.
- Cut potatoes into coins.
- Cut the chicken into small pieces.
- Separately, toss each of the above in butter, Montreal seasoning, garlic powder, and dried minced onion. Then, distribute evenly into baking sheet.
- Coat the green beans in the same mixture and distribute into the baking sheet.
- Bake for 30 min until potatoes are tender and chicken reaches 165 deg.
- Serve with BBQ sauce.
